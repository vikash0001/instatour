What's an InstaTour

☆ Our mission is to help people shoot amazing Instagrams ☆
☆ Discover the most Instagrammable spots ☆
☆ Learn photography techniques and Instagram tips
☆ Enjoy hearing stories about the local area ☆
☆ Each guide is a low level Instagram influencer, the tour goes to 5-7 spots over 90 minutes, half well known, the other half are hidden photographic gems and there's 5-15 people per tour. ☆
